Envisioning the Future

Mother Earth is a Living Being

The Diné view the Earth as an equal, living being that is to be respected and honored. Having this mindset could improve the overall health of the environment when we consider things like how to expand on new energy sources.

Involving Indigenous Voices

Indigenous people have always had a unique connection to nature and the environment. It’s important to involve tribes in discussions of the future of environmental restoration and care.

Many tribes have differing views on how the environment is to be understood. The Diné in particular help us see how we can respect the land and all the good things that come from it. The Diné are still around to teach us how to care for the environment. It’s up to us to listen.
