SihasinHope Sihasin is the Diné Bizaad (Navajo) word for hope. This project imagines the future of environmental restoration through an indigenous lens, inspired by Indigenous Futurism. To find ways of combating climate change and making the Earth a better place, it is necessary to include Indigenous voices, who have been caring for the land long before colonizers came. This site contains information on how Indigenous people lived before colonization and how we can use those lessons as a reference for making a better future. Sihasin is also a reference for those who wish to learn more about their own tribes.

*As the collaborators of this project continue research, there will be more tribes added
